Kingdom Plantae

 

Nematanthus Goldfish Plant

  • Prefers bright but not direct light
  • Keep soil moist but not soggy
  • Keep between 60° - 90° F
  • Use rich fast draining potting soil
  • Use basic plant food @ 1/2 strength

The flower has petals fused into a pouch-like shape, with a small opening. The fancied resemblance of the flower to a goldfish gives the plant its common name, "goldfish plant" or "guppy plant".

Ficus lyrata Fiddle-leaf Fig

  • Prefers bright, filtered light
  • Let dry between thorough waterings
  • Keep between 60° - 80° F
  • Use fast draining potting soil
  • Fertilize with a NPK 3-1-2 blend

The fiddle-leaf fig (Ficus lyrata) is a perfect indoor specimen plant. The plant features very large, heavily veined, violin-shaped leaves that grow upright on a tall plant.

Ficus Elastica Rubber Plant

  • Prefers bright but not direct light
  • Water once soil bcomes slightly dry
  • Keep between 60° - 75° F
  • Use a well-draining and aerated soil
  • Use diluted liquid fertilizer

There are a number of Ficus elastica varieties grown successfully indoors which includes, "the most common one" F.elastica decora, F.elastica robusta and a selection of variegated types.
